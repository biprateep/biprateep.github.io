I am currently an Eric and Wendy Schmidt AI in Science Fellow at the Department of Statistical Sciences and the Vector Institute for Artificial Intelligence at the University of Toronto. I also hold joint appointments at the Canadian Institute for Theoretical Astrophysics (CITA) as a CITA fellow and at the Dunlap Institute for Astronomy and Astrophysics as a Dunlap Fellow.

My work involves developing statistical machine-learning tools to study the formation and evolution of the Universe and the galaxies within. I also work on designing large astronomical sky surveys which enable such statistical studies and am “builder” of the Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument (DESI) collaboration. I am also passionate about developing a scientific community that is accessible and welcoming to all.
